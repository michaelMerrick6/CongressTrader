## CongressTrader
CongressTrader is an iOS app that tracks stock trades made by members of the US Congress. It helps users see who is buying or selling stocks and allows them to follow specific politicians.

## Features
* **Search:** You can search for trades by Politician Name (Pelosi) or Stock Ticker (AAPL).
* **Leaderboard:** Shows a ranked list of politicians who trade the most money.
* **Following:** You can follow politicians or stocks to add them to your personal feed.
* **Save Data:** The app remembers who you follow even if you close the app (uses UserDefaults).
* **Clean Data:** Automatically fixes different name formats.

## How to Run the App
1.  Download or Clone this repository.
2.  Open the file `congressTrader.xcodeproj` in Xcode.
3.  Make sure the `trades.csv` file is inside the project folder.
4.  Select **iPhone 16** as the simulator.
5.  Click play.

## Requirements
* Xcode 15 or higher
* iOS 16.0 or higher
